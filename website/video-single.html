Skills Deep Dive

Skills System Masterclass

The Skills system is what separates a capable AI agent from a great one. Without Skills, your agent rediscovers your site from scratch every session — querying the database, reading config files, re-learning your custom post types. With Skills, that knowledge is encoded once and loaded automatically at session start.

In this 18-minute masterclass, you'll learn how the Skills system works under the hood, how to write great Skills that your agent actually uses, and how to build a session-start protocol that loads the right Skills automatically.

We cover: Skill YAML frontmatter, the enable_agentic and enable_prompt flags, naming conventions, and patterns for team Skills vs. site-specific Skills. We also walk through three real Skills from a production WPCodex installation.

Prerequisites: WPCodex installed and connected to Claude Desktop. Familiarity with basic Markdown helps.
