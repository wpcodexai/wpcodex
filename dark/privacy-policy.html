Privacy Policy

How WPCodex collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Who we are

WPCodex is developed and maintained by wpcodex ("we", "us", "our"). Our website is wpcodex.com. You can contact us at privacy@wpcodex.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: name and email address when you create a Pro or Agency account.
  • Payment data: billing information processed by our payment provider (we do not store card numbers).
  • Usage data: analytics on website visits (pages viewed, referrer, browser, country) collected via a privacy-respecting analytics tool — no cross-site tracking.
  • Support communications: emails and messages you send to our support team.
  • Plugin data: the WPCodex plugin itself runs on your WordPress server. We do not collect or have access to data on your WordPress site, your agent's actions, or your Skills content.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve WPCodex and related services.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To send product updates and announcements (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) to handle transactions.
  • Email service providers to deliver transactional emails.
  • Analytics providers using privacy-respecting, cookieless tools.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies (for session management and security) and optional analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details. You can control cookie preferences via the banner on your first visit.

6. Data retention

We retain account data for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days. Support communications are retained for 2 years. Analytics data is retained for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@wpcodex.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data — TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage, and access controls limited to authorised personnel. No system is 100% secure; if you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Children

WPCodex is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover we have done so, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via email or a notice on this page. Continued use of WPCodex after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@wpcodex.com.
