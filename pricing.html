Pricing FAQ

Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The core plugin is MIT-licensed open source. All core abilities are free with no time limit or usage cap.
Can I cancel Pro at any time?
Yes. Cancel any time from your account. Pro abilities will stop working at the end of the billing period; your data and skills remain intact.
Do you offer a free trial for Pro?
Yes — 14-day free trial on Pro. No credit card required to start.
What counts as a "site"?
One WordPress installation with WPCODEX_ENABLE_MCP active. Each subsite in a multisite network counts as one site unless you're on the Agency plan.
